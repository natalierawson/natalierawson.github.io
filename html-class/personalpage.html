Hobbies

Music is kind of my main thing at this point, so on a certain level I wonder if I can list anything about it as a hobby.

Aside from that, I mess around online a lot. I'm kind of immersed in online culture. God, I cringed typing that, but I think I can justify it. I think that a lot of the strange things you find online can be rooted in already established philosophical and artistic concepts. A lot of the memes you see today are very reminiscent of the Commedia dell'Arte, for example. On the other end of the spectrum, a lot of the bizarre and strange videos you find, I believe, echo the Dadaist period. Or so I tell myself to help believe it isn't all just a waste of time. Which would just be nihilism, so I'm covered.
