The Magic of Music Summer Creative Arts Camp

Summer Sessions

The two-week camps offer something for everyone who loves music, and flexible scheduling to accommodate vacation plans and parents' schedules. No previous music instruction required.

Session 1 Wed, June 21 - Fri, June 30
Session 2 Wed, July 5 - Fri, July 14
Session 3 Mon, July 17 - Fri, July 28
Session 4 Mon, July 31 - Fri, August 11
Session 5 Mon, August 14 - Fri, August 25

Kids' Camp 5 to 12yo.

Fill your child's summer with the welcome sounds of music and laughter! No previous music instruction required.

Join us in this two-week introduction to the magic of music. Our fun and engaging camp includes music appreciation, musical theatre, singing and movement, music games, arts and crafts, and much more! Students will explore different instruments including piano, guitar, recorder, drums, and many more. In our inspiring musical theatre classes, children will sing, dance, act, and design and make props and costumes for a student-inspired and student-created musical theatre performance - merging visual arts, musicality, and social elements for an exciting final presentation. Qualified professionals help bring out your child's individuality in a fun and creative environment by leading small elective classes in small groups designed to promote individual attention.

Extended Day Program (Optional)

Students will design their own instruments, learn about sound properties, natural harmonics and their relationship to the strings, and much more!

Music Counselors in Training (CIT) program 13 to 16yo.

For teens ages 13 to 16 who love music, theater arts, and helping others! Our program offers a chance to work closely with experienced artistic professionals in a warm, supportive environment for children. The two-week camp format allows time to practice a variety of skills necessary for helping young children learn while having fun, focusing on positive reinforcement, setting reasonable expectations, appreciating differences, classroom etiquette, and much more. At the end of each two-week session, participants receive a "Counselor in Training" certificate, and have the opportunity to receive recommendations from professional Keys for Kids staff.

Discount

We recognize that many children best enjoy attending vacation camps with a buddy. Encourage your children to bring a friend to camp and earn a "friendly" discount when you register your child and a new camper at the same time. To take advantage of this offer, simply:

  1. Register your child and a "new" camper (a child who has not previously attended our Summer Arts Program) at the same time.
  2. Be sure to check off the box marked "Friendly discount, please!" and fill in the two campers' names on the adjacent line.
  3. Receive free registration for both campers!
