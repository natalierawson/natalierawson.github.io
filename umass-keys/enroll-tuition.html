We accept checks, cash, or major credit cards.

New Students

Payment is due at the time of enrollment. An enrollment form submitted without deposit is considered incomplete, and the student will not be assigned to a class or lesson time.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We have a full month or 4 lesson satisfaction guarantee for all new students enrolled for a full semester (18 weeks). We're confident that you will love your classes at Keys for Kids, but if you decide that the program is not a good fit for any reason, we will issue you a full tuition refund, minus the cost of the first 4 lessons.

Continuing Students

A complete semester payment (or $100 deposit per student) is due on May 10 for Fall Semester & December 10 for Spring Semester. An enrollment form submitted without deposit is considered incomplete and the student will not be assigned to a class or lesson time. The remaining balance is due by August 10 for Fall Semester & January 10 for Spring Semester. Email reminders will be sent until the tuition is paid in full. Tuition bills that are 10 days overdue are assessed a $25.00 late fee. Students with tuition payments 20 days overdue are not eligible to attend classes and will receive a discontinuation notice.

Tuition

Tuition Includes

  • 36 weeks per school year divided into two 18-week semesters for group classes / three 12-week trimesters for private lessons
  • All group class materials: method books, CDs, flash cards, materials bag for new students
  • Performances, recitals, and concerts
  • Access to practice rooms
  • Special events & masterclasses
  • Parent education and support

Discounts

Family Discounts

  • $50 off the tuition is given for families with 2 or more enrolled students (assumes full semester enrollment for each child = 18 weeks)
  • Keys for Tots (ages 6 months up to 3 years) class sibling discount: Siblings under 6 months old join free of charge; Two siblings age 6 mos.+ join for the price of one; A third sibling may be added for a discounted $125 tuition.

Music Immersion Discounts

$50 off the tuition for students enrolled in multiple programs (assumes full semester enrollment = 18 weeks).

Referral Discounts

Refer a friend and you both receive $50 tuition credit. Tuition credit will apply to the first tuition period after the new student's enrollment.

Private Book Deposits

Keys for Kids faculty members handle the ordering and purchasing of music and other materials for private students to avoid delays in acquiring diverse materials needed for private lessons. Private students are required to make an initial $50 book deposit once per year. A record will be kept of all music expenses and other miscellaneous fees. When the balance falls below $10, you will be asked to bring your account up to a credit balance of $50. If a student should terminate lessons, any unused deposits in this account will be refunded.
