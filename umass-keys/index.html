Keys for Kids

This site was completed during Fall 2016 for my Topics in Web Development: Adobe Dreamweaver class with UMass Lowell. The class was given a list of possible sites to give a full redesign. Keys for Kids was my selection.

The goals for the project were to:

  1. Update the structure of the page so that it adheres to modern web standards.
  2. Create a more accessible navigation scheme.
  3. Implement an more pleasing color scheme.
  4. Reorganize the site content with regards to usability.
  5. Create a sitemap.
  6. Create low-fi wireframes to illustrate the flow of content on every page.
  7. Create two mockups of possible design choices for the index page.

Note: Content on project reflects the content on the original site as of November 2016.

Private And Group Music Lessons For All Ages

Whether you want to compete in prestigious competitions or play an instrument just for the joy of making music, Keys for Kids will help you and your child achieve your goals. With dynamic programs and outstanding teachers for every age, Keys for Kids maintains the highest standards of excellence in a warm, supportive environment that focuses on the joy of music. Shy or star, your child will be motivated and involved with hands-on, personalized instruction.
