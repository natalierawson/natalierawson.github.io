Private Lessons (all ages, all instruments)

Private instructions are offered to students of all ages and levels. Private instructions provide an individually tailored curriculum for a great opportunity to learn to play an instrument or to continue building on existing knowledge and skill levels. Students learn technique, music interpretation and repertoire, music theory, duets/ensembles and improvisation, as well as discipline and efficient practicing tools. Lessons include an array of traditional methods carefully chosen by the teacher to meet individual goals and needs.

Many opportunities are available to students who want to perform: Keys for Kids holds semi-annual studio Recitals (winter and spring), a studio Showcase concert, and a Piano Proficiency Evaluation for interested students. Additional opportunities include various local and national competitions and evaluations. New students are welcome throughout the year whenever space is available.

Each student is matched with the compatible teacher after an initial placement interview. Please contact us to schedule a tour of the school, plan the trial lesson and a meeting with the instructor. Be sure to let us know time that works with your regular schedule - That way you will meet your teacher!

Terms: One lesson per week, three 12-week trimesters per school year

Special Needs Students

Individual weekly private lessons provide opportunities for children with special needs to experience self-expression through active exploration of the instrument and voice. Central to these lessons is the use of familiar and preferred music. Combined with music theory, a music therapy lesson uses the musical preferences and ability of the student to guide the learning process.Parent participation is an important part of this class.

Terms: One lesson per week, three 12-week trimesters per school year

Summer Private Lessons

As always, private lessons are offered throughout the summer.

Many students excel in the more relaxed atmosphere of the summer. It is a wonderful time to continue the progress you have made during the school year or to "catch up" if you feel you have fallen behind.

Private students might choose to continue their lessons in July and August. It is a great way to keep up with skills acquired during the school year and a wonderful opportunity to learn some new, fun, and exciting music.

The schedule for summer private lessons is very flexible, and can be planned in advance with your instructor to accommodate your availability. The tuition for the summer lessons will depend on the number of private lessons you are planning to take. Summer private lessons are offered in packages of four, six, or eight lessons. Students will have plenty of time for summer fun and activities while maintaining their passion for music!
