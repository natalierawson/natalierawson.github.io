Welcome

If you are reading this, when you were a child and your mother was expecting, your father stole from my garden. It was a great personal loss. In turn, I put a curse on him to be the end of his family line. I also took custody of your new baby sister, but that's another story. Your father got off easy (I could have turned him into a dog, or a stone, or a chair or a sna...you get it) and any attempts you have made to have a baby have been unsuccessful as a result.

I, in my generosity, have offered you a chance to undo the curse.

What's in it for me, you ask? Shut up.

Yes, you have a sister. Shut up.

There is a potion you must make for me which consists of four key ingredients:

  1. A cow as white as milk.
  2. A cape as red as blood.
  3. Hair as yellow as corn.
  4. A slipper as pure as gold.

You're likely to find them in the nearby wood, but be quick! I must have these ingredients before midnight in three days for the potion to work.

For your convenience, I have written about the ingredients in plain English and basic Javascript. I don't know why I expect you to understand Javascript. I know you are a baker and come from a family of bakers. Sorry about your father's tragic "Baking Accident" by the way.

Cow as White as Milk

It's a cow. I need it to be white. Preferably live, but I can make do in a pinch. Are you familiar with cows? Bos taurus. You can probably find something at the market. Boys part with their cows for the queerest of prices.

var cow = {color:"white"};
Cape as Red as Blood

It's a coat for people who don't know how to sew sleeves. Doesn't matter from who. It doesn't matter what you need to do or who you have to upset in order to get it. Bring me that cape. Yes, the color matters. I want red. I don't do salmon or orange or burnt sienna. The darker red the better. Like you're looking for a lost innocence or death allegory.

var cape = {color: "red"};
Hair as Yellow as Corn

Yellow hair. Keep away from towers. What else could you possibly need to know? Is this kingdom entirely devoid of blonds? This should be the easiest item on the list for you, really.

var hair = {color:"yellow"};
