About

I hold a BM in Vocal Performance from the Boston Conservatory at Berklee from 2014 (For more about my musical life you can visit my performance page.) and a Certificate in Web Design and Web Development from UMass Lowell from 2017. While design and marketing are newer to me than my base in music, I've found that the rigorous logical, organizational, linguistic, and research skills that I've cultivated to be successful in that area of my life have leant themselves very easily as I grow to embrace other fields in the work force.

Through UMass, I have completed a variety of projects. From my time with the school, I am most proud of her assignment to redesign and build a new website for Keys for Kids, a local music school. I've also enjoyed self-learning environments. To supplement my certificate, I've been able to complete courses with Lynda, Udemy, and CodeAcademy.

Outside of school, I've also designed, proposed, and built a hub for the reference section of Duxbury Free Library's website. I've done work with Bespoke, a marketing company/web shop based in Boston, MA. With Bespoke, I had a taste of both marketing and coding worlds. I've created logos and generated visual assets, tested websites and created reports for the lead developers, as well as built prototypes for websites for boutique hotels. I'm looking forward to seeing how I can continue to grow and build experience now that I've moved to Birmingham.
